Privacy Policy

At Egoinfinitura, your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website.

Information We Collect

We only collect personal information you voluntarily provide through the registration process, such as your name and email address. This data is used solely to manage your account and is never used for marketing or third-party purposes.

Additionally, we use two functional cookies: one to record your acceptance of our Terms, and another to maintain your logged-in session. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not partner with or share any data with third-party vendors. Your personal data is never sold, traded, or disclosed without your consent.

Data Security

Only authorized administrators have access to user data, strictly for operational and security purposes. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Confidentiality of Forum and Goals Page

Forum messages are anonymous and cannot be linked to individual users. However, they are visible to administrators for security purposes and to monitor for unlawful activities or violations of our terms. Entries made on the goals page are fully private and accessible only to you.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To make such a request, contact us at behj8622@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed of any changes.
